Max Temperature

We can see that in the Northern hemisphere that max temp has a strong downward trend for cities located further to the North. On the other hand, while a similar (but of course opposite) trend can be seen in the Southern hemisphere, the difference is much smaller between cities of different latitudes. Most likely this is explained by the fact that it is currently winter in the Northern hemisphere and summer in the Southern, so the Southern hemisphere is experiencing warmer weather.
